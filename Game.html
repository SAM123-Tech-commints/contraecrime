Get Started

Welcome to CyberLaw Quiz Master! This interactive quiz tests your knowledge of cybercrime laws in the Philippines and globally. Select a difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard) and answer questions within the time limit. Earn points for correct answers and learn through detailed explanations.

Tip: Read each question carefully and use the timer wisely to maximize your score!

Power-Ups & Gameplay

Each difficulty level has a different time limit: Easy (60s), Medium (45s), or Hard (30s). You can use one power-up per round:

  • Extra Time 1 & 2: Adds 10 seconds to the timer.
  • Time Freeze: Pauses the timer for strategic thinking.

Note: Only one power-up can be used per round, so choose strategically!

Scoring & Redemption

Earn 10 points for each correct answer in the main round and 5 points in the redemption round. After each question, review the explanation to deepen your understanding. If you miss questions, a redemption round lets you retry up to 3 for extra points.

Pro Tip: Use the Exit Game button to end the quiz early and view your results!
