How to Play CyberLaw Quiz Master

Quiz Overview

Test your knowledge of cybercrime laws in the Philippines and globally. Answer questions on legal provisions across Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulties.

Power-Ups & Gameplay

Choose a difficulty: Easy (60s), Medium (45s), or Hard (30s). Use one power-up per round: Extra Time (+10s, twice) or Time Freeze (pause timer, once). Questions are randomized.

Scoring & Redemption

Earn 10 points per correct answer, 5 in redemption. Review explanations after each question. Redeem up to 3 wrong answers in a bonus round. Exit anytime!
