How to Play CyberLaw Quiz Master

Quiz Overview

Welcome to the CyberLaw Quiz Master! Test your knowledge of cybercrime laws in the Philippines and beyond.

  • Answer multiple-choice questions on legal provisions.
  • Choose from three difficulty levels: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  • Earn points for correct answers and learn from detailed explanations.

Gameplay Mechanics

Navigate through questions with a timer based on your chosen difficulty:

  • Easy: 60 seconds per question.
  • Medium: 45 seconds per question.
  • Hard: 30 seconds per question.
  • Questions are randomized for a unique experience.

Power-Ups & Scoring

Strategize with power-ups, usable once per round:

  • Extra Time 1 & 2: Add 10 seconds each.
  • Time Freeze: Pause the timer once.
  • Scoring: 10 points per correct answer, 5 in redemption.
  • Celebrate correct answers with confetti!

Redemption & Results

Maximize your score with the redemption round:

  • Answer up to one question from mistakes to earn 5 points.
  • View detailed results with accuracy, time, and analysis.
  • Exit anytime using the "Exit Game" button.
